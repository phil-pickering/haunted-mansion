You let Melissa down and shoo her away. She looks up reproachfully and scampers off into the woods. You and your cousins head back to their house.

That evening you ask your aunt and uncle whether they have heard anything about a reward for ending the curse of Chimney Rock. Your uncle laughs at the idea, but he says that if anyone knows, it would be the Bigley’s family lawyer, Gilliam Prem.
