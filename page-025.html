You continue down the stairs. As you take your first step into the kitchen, you see a man peering through a window. He is a large, heavy man and his square jaw and squinty eyes give him a sinister appearance. He surveys the room and his gaze rests upon you. It is Jervis, the caretaker.

Although you stand motionless in the shadows, you are certain he has seen you. In a moment, he leaves the window. He must be coming around to the back door.
