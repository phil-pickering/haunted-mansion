Next morning you thank your cousins and say good-by.

A couple of weeks later you receive from Jane a most amazing letter. She says that Mrs. Bigley’s will provided that, if the cat chose a new owner who was willing to keep it, such person would become the owner of the cat—and of Chimney Rock. Because they kept Melissa, Michael and Jane now own Chimney Rock, and, because the witch’s curse is ended, it’s worth a quarter of a million dollars!
