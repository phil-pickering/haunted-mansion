“That’s all right,” you say. “Thanks for your offer.”

“The mistress would not mind you coming in, but she would not want you prowling around,” he says. “Do you understand? So you must leave here now—or I shall make you regret you ever came!”

You hurry away. Jervis watches until you reach the road before going back into the cottage.
