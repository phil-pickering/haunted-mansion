You yell for help, but there is no answer. Hours go by. Every few minutes you yell and pound on the door. Darkness falls, and you sit helplessly, wondering if you will ever be rescued. Finally, you fall asleep.

When you awaken, if you are not still dreaming, the room seems to have expanded a hundredfold. It is entirely empty, except, a few yards away, sleeping quietly, is a mouse the same size as you.
