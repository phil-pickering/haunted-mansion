“I’m sorry to intrude,” you say, “but I must find my cousin Jane. She was here a moment ago and now she is missing.”

“Oh, you needn’t worry about your little friend,” Mrs. Bigley says. “She must have run downstairs. We’ll go and look for her.”

Relieved at Mrs. Bigley’s words, you start down the stairs, thinking she will follow. After a few steps you glance back. She is gone. Confused, you continue on to the first floor and into the library. It looks as if you will have to search the house on your own.
