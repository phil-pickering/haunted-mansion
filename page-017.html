“No thanks,” you reply. “I had heard this house is occupied only by Mrs. Bigley’s cat. But I guess Mrs. Bigley still lives here too. If I’d known that, I wouldn’t have barged in.”

“Oh, Mrs. Bigley doesn’t mind. I’m sure she was expecting you. She loves to have visitors,” Lena says.

“I’m glad of that,” you reply. “But where is she?”

“She is upstairs in the music room, playing the piano,” Lena answers.
