Suddenly, Mrs. Krim looks up, her face taut with fear.

“Run to the window and jump!” she cries. “Jump for your life!”

You have no time to think. You throw open the window and jump, landing on the ground with a terrible thud. A shooting, stabbing pain flashes through your left arm. Michael and a policeman come running up.

“You have a broken arm,” the policeman tells you a moment later. “That marks you as another victim of the curse of Chimney Rock.”
