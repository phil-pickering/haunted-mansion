You tear down the stairs, but in your haste you trip and fall, smashing your head on a post at the foot of the banister. You sit dazed for a moment, rubbing the growing lump on your forehead.

Pulling yourself together, you make your way outdoors into the welcome sunshine. By the time you return to your own home, your bump has disappeared, but in its place is a reddish mark about the size of a half dollar, the mark of the witch’s curse!
