“Just a minute,” you say to Lena. You run back to the front hall. Jane is there.

“I’m free!” she cries, and she runs up and hugs you.

In a moment Lena joins you.

“I know where Mrs. Bigley keeps the key to the front door,” she says.

She opens a cabinet, gets the key, unlocks the big oak door and flings it open. The three of you go running out onto the front lawn, not noticing that you are being followed by a black cat, which, if you turned to look at it, would seem to be twenty times your size. Fortunately, you don’t look back.
