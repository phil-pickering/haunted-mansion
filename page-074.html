You turn back and walk toward the dark part of the hall near the stairs. A thin young woman in a short black dress beckons you over.

“You must help me,” she whispers. “I’m Mrs. Bigley’s maid, Lena. I can talk to you now because Mrs. Bigley is not watching. I am under a curse. You can save me, but only if . . .” Suddenly, she shrieks.

You look around and see an old woman with a cane standing nearby. She is slim, like Lena, though much older and much taller. She is slightly stooped, and her head swings around like the head of a snake. Her sharp, green eyes fix on you and then on Lena, who seems to shrivel under the force of her mistress’ gaze. So this is Mrs. Bigley!

“Look into my eyes,” the old woman says to you.
